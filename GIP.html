AIESEC

AIESEC is the international platform for young people to explore and develop their potential. Present in 1,700 universities in over 100 countries, it is the largest student organization with 28,000 members worldwide. AIESEC organizes the Global Internship Program, allowing participants to work for a partner company anywhere where AIESEC is present. Through its global learning environment, members develop impactful and global-minded leadership.

Global Internship Program (GIP)

The Global Internship is the most intense learning experience that we offer through our network. Each year, we provide over 5,500 members the challenging opportunity to live and work in a foreign country. Participants can choose to work in areas of management, technology, education, or development. Participating in an AIESEC exchange is especially a unique experience because you will have support for each step along the way; between your local chapter and your host chapter, you will be guided by a strong and stable network. An AIESEC internship is a perfect summer job, an alternative to the Professional Experience Year, or a launching point for your career after graduation.

Details and Logistics

  • Types of Internships: Management, Technical, Education, Development
  • Internship duration: 6 weeks to 18 months
  • Country of Placement: Any country that has an AIESEC committee (over 100 to choose from)
  • Salary: Management and Technical Internships - the salary will cover living costs.

1. Selection and Application

After joining AIESEC and assuming an active role, you may start your application process. During this stage, your suitability in terms of motivation and ability to adapt will be assessed. This ensure that participants will have a positive experience while abroad on an AIESEC internship. This step will consist of completing a form and passing the review board interview.

2. Preparation

In order to help guarantee a positive experience, AIESEC provides a mandatory preparation seminar for its GIP candidates. In this stage you will learn about:

  • Logistical preparation: Ensuring that you are physically and legally prepared to work in another country (visas etc).
  • Community Involvement: To help you gain an understanding of current global issues and those in your community.
  • Direct Country preparation: How to cope with the pressures of living in a foreign culture, and eventually the country that you will going to.
  • AIESEC preparation: Providing you with knowledge about the exchange program and the roles of various entities.

The preparation stage is an extremely important one as we want our participants to be ready to make the most out of their experiences, both personally and professionally speaking. We also want people to realize their roles and responsibilities when abroad.

3. Matching

By this stage you have passed the selection process and became an official AIESEC exchange participant (EP). You are now able to start looking for your desired internship. You will be trained on and given access to our on-line database of internship opportunities. The more flexible you are in terms of geographical preferences and company preferences, the easier it would be to find a quick and desirable match.

4. Integration

This is the most fulfilling step - experiencing the actual internship itself! Your host AIESEC chapter will provide you with much more information on what to expect during your internship and how you can stay involved with AIESEC and the local environment. They will also provide support for your arrival, finding a place to stay, opening a bank account, applying for temporary residency and taking you to your first day of work while providing opportunities to participate cultural and social activities.

5. Re-Integration

Your local AIESEC chapter will support you readjusting when you return home. AIESEC offers a wealth of opportunities for after you complete your internship. Try taking on a leadership position in the organization, participating in international conferences or networking with alumni and partners.
