AIESEC

AIESEC is the international platform for young people to explore and develop their potential. Present in 1,700 universities in over 100 countries, it is the largest student organization with 28,000 members worldwide. AIESEC organizes the Global Internship Program, allowing participants to work for a partner company anywhere where AIESEC is present. Through its global learning environment, members develop impactful and global-minded leadership.

Exchange Program

AIESEC, the world's largest youth-driven organization, is the international platform for young people to explore and develop their leadership potentials and create positive impacts on society. AIESEC offers young people the opportunity to participate in international internships, experience leadership and partake in a global learning environment. Exchange is the core product of AIESEC, which was brought up in 1948 after World War II with the aim to creating a new generation of young leaders, who would emerge from university with the cultural sensitivity and responsibility of rebuilding their countries
